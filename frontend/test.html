NikaBet Support Chat — Test Page

This page embeds the floating AI support widget (bottom-right) — open it and chat to test the bot end-to-end.

What this service does

  • AI customer-support chat for NikaBet (casino + sportsbook), answering from a per-topic knowledge base.
  • Answers follow the player's language automatically; the widget chrome starts in the browser language.
  • Routes questions to the right topic, suggests follow-up questions, and offers a "finish chat" action when resolved.
  • Escalates to human support (contact button) on request, complaints, suspected fraud, or when it can't help.
  • Proactive retention pings (Telegram): admin-managed inactivity rules send AI-written re-engagement messages, with hard anti-spam caps, quiet hours, and a /stop opt-out.
  • Anti-spam: rate limiting, cooldown, low-content and prompt-injection guards before any model call.
  • Admin dashboard — the React Admin SPA served at /admin over the /admin/* API: metrics, sessions, and knowledge-base editing.
